About Me (Google Scholar)

I am currently a MPhil Candidate at TML Lab, University of Sydney, Australia, supervised by Prof. Tongliang Liu and co-supervised by Prof. Mingming Gong. I will graduate in 07/2022 and participate in TML Lab as a Ph.D candidate. From 01/2020 to 10/2020, I was a research intern at Institute of Deep Learning, Baidu Research, supervised by Guodong Guo. From 02/2021 to 09/2021, I was a research intern at Y-tech, Kuaishou Technology, supervised by Qiang Li. Now, I am a research intern at OPPO Research Institute, supervised by Yandong Guo. We have several intern positions at OPPO Research Institute. We actively work on dense prediction, self-supervised learning, and multi-modal learning. If you like what we do, don't hesitate to contact me.

Research Interests

    My research mainly focuses on Visual Self-supervised Learning, Multi-modal Learning, Label-noise Learning, and Dense Prediction.
